$D_{KL}(q(w|\Gamma, \theta_1) || p(w, \Gamma|h_1)) = D_{KL}(q(w|\Gamma, \theta_1) || p(w, \Gamma|h_1, \lambda_1)) = D_{KL}(q(w|\Gamma, \theta_1) || p(w, \Gamma|h_1, \lambda'))$;

$D_{KL}(q(w|\Gamma, \theta_2) || p(w, \Gamma|h_2)) = D_{KL}(q(w|\Gamma, \theta_2) || p(w, \Gamma|h_2, \lambda_2)) = D_{KL}(q(w|\Gamma, \theta_2) || p(w, \Gamma|h_2, \lambda'))$.

ѕодставл€€ решени€ указанных задач, которые по условию €вл€ютс€ единственными, получаем:

$\lambda_{likelihood}^Q E_{q(w,\Gamma|\theta_1)}\log{p(y|X, w, \Gamma)} - \lambda_{prior\ 1}^Q D_{KL}(q(w|\Gamma, \theta_1) || p(w, \Gamma|h_1, \lambda')) + \log{p(h_1|\lambda_1)} >\\ 
> \lambda_{likelihood}^Q E_{q(w,\Gamma|\theta_2)}\log{p(y|X, w, \Gamma)} - \lambda_{prior\ 1}^Q D_{KL}(q(w|\Gamma, \theta_2) || p(w, \Gamma|h_2, \lambda')) + \log{p(h_2|\lambda_2)}$ а также

$\lambda_{likelihood}^Q E_{q(w,\Gamma|\theta_2)}\log{p(y|X, w, \Gamma)} - \lambda_{prior\ 2}^Q D_{KL}(q(w|\Gamma, \theta_2) || p(w, \Gamma|h_2, \lambda')) + \log{p(h_2|\lambda_2)} >\\

—ложим неравенства и отнимем одинаковые слагаемые от обеих частей, получим:

$- \lambda_{prior\ 1}^Q D_{KL}(q(w|\Gamma, \theta_1) || p(w, \Gamma|h_1, \lambda')) - \lambda_{prior\ 2}^Q D_{KL}(q(w|\Gamma, \theta_2) || p(w, \Gamma|h_2, \lambda')) >\\ > - \lambda_{prior\ 1}^Q D_{KL}(q(w|\Gamma, \theta_2) || p(w, \Gamma|h_2, \lambda')) - \lambda_{prior\ 2}^Q D_{KL}(q(w|\Gamma, \theta_1) || p(w, \Gamma|h_1, \lambda'))$.

$(\lambda_{prior\ 1}^Q - \lambda_{prior\ 2}^Q) D_{KL}(q(w|\Gamma, \theta_1) || p(w, \Gamma|h_1)) <
